FAQs

General Frequently Asked Questions
1 Is it safe to invest in $AT?

Yes, it is safe.

2 What is AT Finance?

AT Finance is a technology that is developed to achieve the vision of Binance Smart Chain (BSC) that were meant to step in by creating a clever, strong, and easy to use banking system that can be accessed by anybody in the crypto world.

3 How much is $AT total token supply and will it be stable?

$AT max token supply will be 100,000,000. Due to that token supply, it will be deflationary.

4 Why does AT Finance need blockchain technology?

AT Finance needs blockchain to be able to validate the data that comes in and goes out.

5 Is AT Finance a Stablecoin?

No. Stablecoins have a fixed value or fluctuate around it (usually pegged to fiat coins such as USD, EUR, etc). AT Finance is a BEP20-compliant token that runs on the Binance SmartChain

6 What is AT Finance's mission?

AT Finance believes that a vital foundation for global, distributed economies would be blockchain-based markets. $AT tokens are produced and incorporated into the scheme, reflecting the mission of completely implementing BEP20-compliant technology into the market.
