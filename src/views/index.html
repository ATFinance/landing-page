Deflationary Token

AT Finance has a unique burning mechanism automatically sent to burn address with every transaction. Burning tokens essentially eliminate them from the available stock, making them more expensive.

Burning Cycle

When a token is excluded from the current pool of cryptocurrencies in circulation, it is burned. Thus, $AT will run for a total of 148 cycles.

Burn Rate

As people transact, the sum of tokens in your wallet will continue to grow indefinitely. Therefore, the burn rate for AT Finance is from 5% to 12%.

Rebase

This burn will cause a positive rebase when the total burned tokens exceed the target threshold and distributing 50% of the burnt supply to token holders.

General Frequently Asked Questions
1 What is AT Finance?

AT Finance is a small ecosystem created by 6 DAPPs on the Binance Smart Chain Network, which contains an NFT Marketplace, a Liquidity Aggregator platform, a Farming & Staking platform, a BSC Tokens Launchpad, and other two platform waiting to be disclosed which is developed based in Indonesia and Singapore.

2 Where can I buy $AT?

$AT can be bought at UniCrypt during Pre-Sale starting on 18th May 2021 and purchased afterward in PancakeSwap.

3 What is $AT total token supply, and will it be stable?

$AT max token supply will be 100,000,000. Due to that token supply, it will be deflationary.

4 What is the slippage tolerance need to buy $AT?

The slippage is between 5% to 15%.

5 How many burning cycles will it be in AT Finance, and what is the burn rate of AT Finance that impacts rebase positively?

There will be a total of 148 burning cycles in the circulation of 5% to 12% burning rate, and this burn rate will impact a positive rebase when the total burned tokens exceed the target threshold and distributing 50% of the burnt supply to token holders.
